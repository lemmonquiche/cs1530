About Class Grouper

The idea was simple: why do students taking and instructors teaching project based classes need to spend time and effort arranging students into groups, forcing new contacts and co-ordinating chaos, instead of focusing on the projects and the courses?

  • Sign up in the beginning of the semester
  • Join a class, join a group, or be assigned one
  • Instantly exchange information and start collaborating
  • Move on with your course work

A Platform For All

Class Grouper is intended to serve the needs of Universities, Instructors, and Students alike, providing features like ease of installation and maintenance, simple student management, and realtime updates to take advantage of class time starting right from syllabus week.
